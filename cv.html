Shuya Iwamatsu received the B. E. and M. E. degrees in electronic engineering from Osaka University, Osaka, Japan, in 2020 and 2021. His bachelor and master degrees concentrated on developing resonant tunneling diode (RTD) oscillators for terahertz (THz) wireless communications. He was the recipient of the Runner-Up at the Best Student Paper Award of the IRMMW-THz in 2020. He is currently working toward the Ph. D. degree on photonics-assisted terahertz beam steering system using leaky-wave antenna with the Optoelectronics department, University of Duisburg-Essen, Duisburg, Germany.
